Fast, Reliable, AI-powered

Our AI model analyzes MRI scans in seconds to detect Alzheimer's stages. Helping doctors, patients, and researchers worldwide with early detection and diagnosis support.

Why Early Detection Matters?

Early diagnosis enables timely treatment, future planning, improved quality of life, and access to clinical trials aimed at slowing disease progression.

About the Project

Alzheimer's Detector leverages transfer learning with VGG16 to classify MRI images into multiple Alzheimer's stages. It simplifies the clinical workflow by providing instant preliminary analysis, saving time and improving patient outcomes.
